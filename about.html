Profile

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer who focuses on working on simplifying interfaces and I’m not afraid to design apps for any kind of devices, and platforms. I love to collaborate, facilitate discussions with users and I have lots of energy :)

My true passion is designing aesthetic, human-friendly interfaces. I discovered it after I had completed my Master's degree in architecture and after few years of working in my profession. My time in Architecture has helped me develop aesthetic thinking and has given me a great eye for details. Combined with my UX skills I am able to emphatise with users. I also like to design logos, icons, illustrations and 3D visualizations. Working with me, you can be sure that I put all my heart into it and that I am open to every new challenge.
